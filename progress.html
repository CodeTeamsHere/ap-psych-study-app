How mastery levels work

New

Not attempted yet.

Practiced

Attempted, but not yet 70% rolling accuracy (or too few questions).

Familiar

≈70–85% rolling accuracy.

Proficient

≈80%+ sustained over 5+ recent questions.

Mastered

≈90%+ sustained over 8+ recent questions. Accuracy can drop you back down, so it stays honest.

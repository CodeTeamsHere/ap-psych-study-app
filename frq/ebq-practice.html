Evidence-Based Question (EBQ)

You'll read three summarized peer-reviewed sources on one topic and answer three parts (A–C) for 7 points in ~45 minutes. Two original, exam-style sets are below.

🧭 How to approach it
  • A — state a clear, defensible claim that answers the question (1 pt).
  • B — cite specific evidence from one source (1 pt) and explain/apply a psychological concept to it (2 pts).
  • C — cite different evidence from a different source (1 pt) and apply a different psychological concept (2 pts).
  • Cite as "(Source A)" or "According to Source B…". The applied concept must be one explicitly in the AP Psychology CED.

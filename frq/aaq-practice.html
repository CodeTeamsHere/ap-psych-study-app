Article Analysis Question (AAQ)

You'll read one summarized peer-reviewed study and answer six parts (A–F) for 7 points in ~25 minutes. Two original, exam-style sets are below. Write your response, then reveal the rubric and model answer to self-score.

🧭 How to approach it
  • A — name the research method (experiment, correlational, case study, meta-analysis, etc.).
  • B — give an operational definition: how a variable was actually measured.
  • C — interpret a specific statistic in the context of the study.
  • D — name an ethical guideline the researchers followed (informed consent, debriefing, confidentiality…).
  • E — explain generalizability using the actual participant sample.
  • F — use a specific result to explain support for/against the hypothesis (worth 2).
